We're building a free network service that will let you share and discover new music.

Libre.fm is a project to help you keep track of what music you like and share that, with your friends, discover new music, and meet new people who share your taste in music.

  • Complete public source code licensed under the GNU AGPL!
  • You own your own data!
  • Use our service, or run your own!
  • Get started and hacking today!

We're still in the alpha stages of the project, but many users and developers are actively using Libre.fm to share their listening habits.

  • See what people are listening to
  • Join the project at Savannah
  • Browse our source code or check out a copy for yourself
  • Add your ideas to our ideas wiki
  • Join our mailing list or just browse the archives
